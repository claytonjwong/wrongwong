Stress

Damn right I like the life I live 'Cause I went from negative to positive.

The Notorious B.I.G., "Juicy" from "Notorious" soundtrack

Bad stress causes me to experience additional fatigue, brain fog, and inflammation. I avoid bad stress by focusing on conflict resolution and focusing forward on the day to avoid dwelling and ruminating upon previous stressful experiences. It's easy to get caught up in a negative cycle. The sooner I abscond the negativity cyclone, the better.

Stay Positive

When one's expectations are reduced to zero, one really appreciates everything one does have.

Stephen Hawking

After I was diagnosed with an incurable autoimmune disease, it was very easy for me to focus on negative aspects of my life. With proper expectations, I've realized my cup is still more than half-full. I've transformed the side-effects of an autoimmune disease into daily reminders to be thankful for what I have. The daily pain I experience is a constant reminder of my mortality. Those reminders guide me to efficiently prioritize, utilize, appreciate, and take advantage of all the amazing opportunities in my life.

Future

The key to realizing a dream is to focus not on success but on significance, and then even the small steps and little victories along your path will take on greater meaning.

Oprah Winfrey

I was scared of the future and how bad I might end up after I attended an AS meetup in San Diego, CA. I witnessed first-hand the horrible affects of AS. Some folks were fused and unable to turn their heads. Most everyone was in discomfort and pain. I was concerned about lack of mobility and paralysis, since its easier to fracture a fused spine and neck from an accidental fall. There's alot of horrible outcomes, but it is not helpful at all to wonder and dwell upon a potential future. By focusing on a negative potential future, I was always anxious and worried about the future. I became severely depressed and had difficultly with basic communication due to overwhelming anxiety. I've found the best way to predict my future is to create it. I became empowered when I chose to change my mind's attention from the future to the present and focus on the moments of each day. Being present and tuned into "here and now", I found the small things I could do each and every day to proactively create my own future. I can't change the future by thinking about it, so I stopped thinking about it. Similiarly I can't change the past by thinking about it. I previously ruminated daily about what I should have done, which caused the same depressing affect as focusing on the future. Focus on the present day and take action. Don't just think about it, make it happen.
